About Dtc

Who we are?

DETC is an innovative training and management-consulting firm, enabling organizations to make significant growth and sustainable improvement & profit. We provide best practices in training and human assets in a range of management and technical fields. Our innovative training courses and consulting services are designed to provide solutions and accomplish results. We combine experience and updated knowledge with personal creativity to analyze client’s problems and produce practical solutions that meet expectations and achieve goals. Our training and consulting team are committed to help our clients (Private and Public sectors) to become strong, productive, and profitable organizations. Based on global experience and local knowledge, we will help you achieve significant and sustainable improvement. Our main offices are located in Cairo, Egypt, with branches represented by in the Middle East and Canada. This enables us to be closer to our clients providing services of the highest standards.
